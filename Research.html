MASTER'S Thesis

Topic: Thermohydraulic performance improvement of an Insert-fitted double pipe heat exchanger with a new class of MXene dispersed Ionanofluid.

Supervisor: Dr. Khairul Habib, Senior Lecturer, Dept. of Mechanical Engineering, UTP
  • A new class of Ionic Liquid based Nanofluid was formulated and characterized.
  • Thermohydraulic performance of an Insert fitted Double Pipe HEX was investigated Numerically with formulated Ionanofluid.
  • Conventional twisted tape was modified with different geometrically perforated inserts and optimum insert geometry was assessed based on a comparative performance analysis.

Undergraduate Thesis

Topic: Title: An experimental investigation on enhancement of heat transfer using twisted tape inserts with rib turbulators.

Supervisor: Dr. Md. Abu Mowazzem Hossain , Assistant Professor, Dept. of Mechanical Engineering, CUET
  • An experiment was executed to improve heat transfer rate of a close conduit using ribbed insert.
